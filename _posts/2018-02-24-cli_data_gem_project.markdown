"The struggle you're in today is developing the strength you need for tomorrow" - Robert Tew.

  Working on this project has made me read this quote more then one can ever imagine. From the get-go, I was struggling to even decide on what I wanted my gem to be, once I decided and made a repo I put my head down and went into full work mode. I felt confident, flying through the setup, so many ideas of how I want my gem to look and work thinking this is going to be a breeze. Then came time to put it all together. I was following the example video getting ideas, still feeling pretty good, putting my CLI file together, how its all going to work. Once I finished all the CLI pre-work I got into the scraping aspect, all my confidence and self-esteem went right down the drain. I was stuck right away looking at previous labs, searching on google not finding any answers, wanting to just give up but knowing that this happens all the time. I got to a point where I couldn't figure out any more moves and I deleted the repo and started over with a new gem idea. I felt defeated, hoping this new idea would be a better fit for me I began doing the pre-setup. I was not confident, not flying through the setup, just still unsure of myself. Then I ran across the beginning quote and it hit me I told myself  "I know I can do this, I can doing anything". 

  Every day since I seen that quote I read it 10 times when I wake up and it's given me the power to work through the tough issues. I stuck with my second idea and even when things got hard I wasn't going to give up and change again I knew I could finish it. After days and hours of hard work here I am today with a working gem that I am truly proud of. My confidence is back at its high peak and I'm excited to learn more and prove to myself I can work through the hard times and enjoy all the good times.
