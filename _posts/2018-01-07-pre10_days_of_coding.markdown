 Yesterday, while working my current full-time job, my mind was only thinking about one thing, my music cli project I'm working on. I could hardly get anything done. All I wanted to do was get home so I could get back to work on my project. I was going over in my head everything I had done so far not focusing on my current job, realizing that learning to code is taking over my brain, it hit me that this is what I need to be doing. Struggling to get anything done, I get an email #10DaysOfCoding challenge and I'm instantly excited, I sit up in my seat open the email and sign up for the challenge. I then go to my calendar and start adjusting my whole schedule for the 10 days giving me as much time possible to learn to code and I couldn't be more excited. I had missed the first challenge and would not miss the second one. Now, as I wait for the challenge to start I set my goals for the 10 days and continue learning.
