  The one aspect of coding I wasn't expecting is the how much people attention it brings. Literally the first person I told that I am in school for web development instantly asked me if I could do something they've wanted to get done. Now, because I was still fairly new I said: "Oh sure I can do that". Next thing I know it seems like everybody has something they want to be done when they find out you can fix or work on websites. As the nice person I am it's hard to turn people down but I'm starting to learn my time is becoming more and more valuable and I will need to manage it wisely.
