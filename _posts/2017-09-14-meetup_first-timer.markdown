  From the time I started learning to code, until just a few weeks ago, I was terrified to attend a meetup. I have always been so nervouse the first time I go into a new social setting wondering, am I going to make myself look silly not knowing as much as the other people attending. What if they ask me something I dont know, or I think I know but my answer is wrong. What if no one wants to talk to me. These were all my concerns about going to meetups and networking events, but I decided I need to put my fears behind me and just go for it. Three weeks ago I attended my first meetup and when I walked in was greeted with open arms and all those fears had dissapered, it was the greatest feeling I could have had at that point. I was able to talk to many people and make good connections, it was completly different then my fears made it out to be. 
	
	So when it comes to going to meetups dont let you fears get in the way. Everyone there is supportive and you can learn alot and make great connections.
